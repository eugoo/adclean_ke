Setup Instructions

How to Set Up AdClean KE

1

Choose Your Device Type

Android Setup

Follow these steps to configure AdClean KE on your Android device:

  1. Go to Settings > Network & Internet
  2. Tap on Private DNS
  3. Select Private DNS provider hostname
  4. Enter: dns.adclean.co.ke
  5. Tap Save

Your Android device is now protected!

iOS Setup

Follow these steps to configure AdClean KE on your iPhone or iPad:

  1. Go to Settings > Wi-Fi
  2. Tap the i icon next to your connected network
  3. Scroll down and tap Configure DNS
  4. Select Manual
  5. Remove any existing servers and add: dns.adclean.co.ke
  6. Tap Save

Your iOS device is now protected!

Windows Setup

Follow these steps to configure AdClean KE on your Windows computer:

  1. Go to Control Panel > Network and Internet > Network and Sharing Center
  2. Click on Change adapter settings
  3. Right-click your active connection and select Properties
  4. Select Internet Protocol Version 4 (TCP/IPv4) and click Properties
  5. Select Use the following DNS server addresses
  6. Enter:
    • Preferred DNS server: 45.90.28.0
    • Alternate DNS server: 45.90.30.0
  7. Click OK to save

Mac Setup

Follow these steps to configure AdClean KE on your Mac:

  1. Go to System Preferences > Network
  2. Select your active connection and click Advanced
  3. Go to the DNS tab
  4. Click the + button and add: 45.90.28.0
  5. Click + again and add: 45.90.30.0
  6. Click OK and then Apply

Router Setup

Configure AdClean KE on your router to protect all devices on your network:

  1. Access your router's admin panel (usually 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1)
  2. Navigate to Internet Settings or DNS Settings
  3. Change DNS servers to:
    • Primary DNS: 45.90.28.0
    • Secondary DNS: 45.90.30.0
  4. Save changes and restart your router

Note: Router interfaces vary by manufacturer. Check your router's manual for specific instructions.

2

Test Your Setup

After configuring AdClean KE, test if it's working:

  1. Visit a website that normally shows ads (like a news site)
  2. Check if ads are blocked
  3. Visit dnsleaktest.com to verify your DNS

If you still see ads, try clearing your browser cache or restarting your device.

3

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions:

  • Ads still showing: Clear browser cache or try a different browser
  • Connection issues: Restart your device and router
  • Setup not working: Contact our support team for assistance
